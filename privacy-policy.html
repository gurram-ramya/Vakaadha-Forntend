Privacy Policy – Vakaadha

Last updated: [Date]

At Vakaadha, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website, place an order, or interact with us.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details: Name, phone number, email, billing & shipping address
  • Payment information (processed securely by Cashfree/PayU — we do not store your full card/UPI info)
  • Order details: Products purchased, delivery preferences, transaction history
  • Device & usage data: Browser type, IP address, and how you use our website

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Send updates (order confirmations, delivery updates)
  • Improve products and website
  • Share promotions (only if you opt-in)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing of Information

We share only with payment providers (Cashfree/PayU), delivery partners, or legal authorities if required by law. We never sell or rent your data.

4. Data Security

Your payment details are encrypted and processed securely. We take reasonable steps to protect your data but cannot guarantee 100% security online.

5. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary for order processing, legal compliance, and improving services.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Ask for correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of promotional emails/SMS anytime

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies, but some features may not work properly.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted with a new “Last updated” date.
